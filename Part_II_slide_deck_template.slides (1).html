Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the characteristics of flights that experienced delays. The main focus was on the month of the year, the airline carrier, and the types of delays. For this presentation, I start by introducing the Unique Carriers, and then present the Carriers responsible for Delays. Next, I present the pattern of delays on a monthly basis, and finally, I explore the various reasons for delay on a monthly basis. The bar charts are grouped by months, and the type of delay is color-coded, while the line graph depicting reasons for delay is color-coded on the delays.
